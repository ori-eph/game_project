Music Maker

Welcome to Music Maker, an online music simulator that lets you create your own original music recordings! In Music Maker, you have access to two instruments: piano and drums. You can play them by either clicking on the key/drum you want to play or by typing in the input box below. You can also record your piece by pressing the record button. Your notes will be recorded and you will have a list of your recordings below the music maker. Additionally, you can adjust the volume with the volume slider and turn off the simulator with the power button. Have fun playing!
